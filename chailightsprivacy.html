Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 10, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to ChaiLights. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

When you use ChaiLights, we may collect and process the following data:

  • Google Photos Data: We request read-only access to your Google Photos to display past photos taken on the same Hebrew calendar date.
  • Google User Information: We collect your name and email address for authentication purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Authenticate users via Google OAuth.
  • Retrieve and display past photos based on Hebrew calendar dates.

We do not store or share your photos. Your data is processed temporarily during your session.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your data, including:

  • Using OAuth authentication to securely access Google Photos.
  • Not storing personal photos or metadata on our servers.

5. Third-Party Services

ChaiLights integrates with Google Photos and Google OAuth. Your use of these services is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Your Choices

You can:

  • Revoke Google Photos access at any time via your Google Account settings.
  • Request data deletion by contacting us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Any changes will be posted on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at chailights.support@gmail.com.
