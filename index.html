About Jake Shoag

Hi, I'm Jake Shoag, a senior at the Fuchs Mizrachi School and the CEO of LOR Link, a platform that lets students manage their recommendation letters. I love playing soccer and basketball, and I am a leader in Bnei Akiva. I am student at Case Western Reserve University in the College Credit Plus program, and I also used to take classes at Cuyahoga Community College.

I am a member of the Saltzmann Youth Panel, and I have worked as a volunteer for Shontel Brown and George Latimer. I was previously the Secretary and Treasurer of the student council. In the summers, I work as a lifeguard at the Beachwood Family Aquatic Center.

I created this page to highlight two of my projects: (1) Dyslex-Kriyah, a Dyslexia-friendly Hebrew font, and (2) LOR Link, a company that helps students and faculty better manage letters of recommendation. I also have a blog, where I share helpful scripts and notes for AP Statistics and other topics.
