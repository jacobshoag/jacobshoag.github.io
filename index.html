About Jake Shoag

Jake Shoag is a junior at the Fuchs Mizrachi School in Cleveland. He is passionate about innovation and community leadership. Jake is the creator of Dyslex-Kriyah, a dyslexia-friendly Hebrew font, and the CEO of LOR Link, a portal for handling letters of recommendation.

Jake is also active in extracurricular activities. He plays soccer and basketball, and he is a leader in Bnei Akiva. He serves as a lifeguard at the Beachwood Family Aquatic Center.

Additionally, Jake has volunteered for Shontel Brown and George Latimer, and he is a member of the Saltzmann Youth Panel. Previously, Jake served as Secretary and Treasurer of the student council at Fuchs Mizrachi School.
