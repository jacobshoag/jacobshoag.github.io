About Jake Shoag

Hi, my name is Jake Shoag, and I'm a junior at the Fuchs Mizrachi School and the CEO of LOR Link. I love playing soccer and basketball, and I am a leader in Bnei Akiva.

I am a member of the Saltzmann Youth Panel, and I have worked as a volunteer for Shontel Brown and George Latimer. I was previously the Secretary and Treasurer of the student council. In the summers, I work as a lifeguard at the Beachwood Family Aquatic Center.

I created this page to highlight two of my projects: (1) Dyslex-Kriyah, a Dyslexia-friendly Hebrew font, and (2) LOR Link, a company that helps students and faculty better manage letters of recommendation. I hope you will check them out.
